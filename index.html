Welcome to Dungeon Crawler - A Python Pygame Adventure!


Embark on an exciting journey through mysterious dungeons filled with treacherous enemies, hidden treasures, and epic battles in this classic dungeon crawler game created with Python and Pygame.

Features:


- Player Character:

Take control of a courageous character armed with ranged weapons, navigating through challenging dungeons.

- Enemies:

A variety of foes, from menacing orcs to powerful bosses, each with unique challenges and strategies.

- Combat System:

Engage in thrilling combat by utilizing your bow to take down enemies while dodging their attacks. Master the art of precision and timing to emerge victorious.

- Item Collection:

Collect valuable loot such as gold, health potions, and other items that will aid you on your perilous journey.

- HUD (Heads-Up Display):

Keep track of your vital statistics, including health, gold, and more, with a user-friendly heads-up display.

- Menu:

Navigate through a simple and intuitive main menu that allows you to start a new game and adjust settings with ease.

-Settings:

Your preferences are saved, ensuring a seamless gaming experience every time you return to the adventure.

How to Play:

1. Download Dungeon Crawler
2. In the upper right corner, click "Download All" or select all files, right-click, and choose "Download."
3. Unpack the game.
4. Open the main.exe file.

Configuration:


Customize your gaming experience by adjusting settings such as screen resolution, sound, and fullscreen mode either in-game or by editing the config/config.txt file.

Project Structure:


Explore a well-organized project structure with folders containing modules and classes for characters, weapons, backgrounds, enemies, HUD, items, music, and menus. The game's levels are defined in CSV files, and assets like graphics and audio files are neatly organized.
